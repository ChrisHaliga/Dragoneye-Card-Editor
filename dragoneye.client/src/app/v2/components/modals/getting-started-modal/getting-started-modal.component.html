Getting Started with Dragoneye
Welcome to Dragoneye Card Editor!

Create, edit, and manage your custom trading cards with an intuitive, modern interface. This guide will help you get started creating amazing cards in just a few minutes.

Creating Your First Cards
  • Start a New Group: Click "Add New Group" to organize your cards by theme or type
  • Add Cards: Click the "Add Card" button in any group to create new cards
  • Edit Your Cards: Double-click any card to open the card editor and customize its properties
  • Add Card Effects: Use the card editor to add spells, abilities, and special effects to your cards
Managing Your Card Collections
  • Save Your Work: Use File → Save to keep your cards safe (or press Ctrl+S)
  • Save Multiple Versions: Use File → Save As to create different versions of your card sets
  • Load Previous Work: Use File → Load to open card sets you've saved before
  • Share Your Cards: Export your cards as JSON files to share with others
  • Import Collections: Import card sets from JSON files to expand your collection
Working with Your Cards
  • Organize by Groups: Group your cards by element, type, or any theme you like
  • Select Multiple Cards: Hold Shift and click to select several cards at once
  • Move Cards Around: Drag cards between groups or reorder them within a group
  • View Your Cards: Use the zoom controls to get a closer look at your card designs
  • Quick Actions: Right-click any card or group for helpful shortcuts
Work Faster with These Features
  • Bulk Operations: Select multiple cards to duplicate or delete several at once
  • Drag & Drop: Drag cards between groups to reorganize your collection
  • Undo & Redo: Made a mistake? Use Ctrl+Z to undo, or Ctrl+Y to redo changes
  • Quick Selection: Drag to select multiple cards, or Shift+click to add to your selection
  • Focus View: Click "Focus on Card" in the View menu to center a specific card
Essential Keyboard Shortcuts
  • Ctrl+S: Save your card collection
  • Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y: Undo and redo your changes
  • Delete: Remove selected cards
  • Ctrl+D: Duplicate selected cards
  • F1: Open this getting started guide
  • Escape: Clear selection or close dialogs
